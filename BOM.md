To put together this frame you will need:

* Six 10' 2x4s 

* Two 8'  2x4s

* Two sheets of 3/4 Plywood

* Two bricks

* Wood glue

The dimensions of the lumber are not critical, so if 2x4s are not available in your area, the equivalent lumber will work.
